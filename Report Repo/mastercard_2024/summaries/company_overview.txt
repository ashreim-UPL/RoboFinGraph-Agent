Mastercard Incorporated, founded in 1966, is a global leader in the payments industry, providing transaction processing, payment solutions, and value-added services. It connects consumers, financial institutions, merchants, governments, and digital partners across more than 210 countries and territories, enabling secure and seamless electronic payments worldwide. The company’s extensive multi-rail payments network, trusted brands such as Mastercard, Maestro, and Cirrus, and innovative digital capabilities—including digital identity, open banking, and digital currencies—are core strengths. Mastercard’s broad ecosystem, franchise model, and advanced security and data analytics solutions help maintain its competitive edge against rivals like Visa and regional players. Its diverse end markets span North America, Europe, Asia, and emerging regions, serving large financial institutions, merchants, and government agencies. The company continues to capitalize on industry trends like digital payments, real-time transactions, and digital currencies through strategic partnerships and innovative product offerings.
