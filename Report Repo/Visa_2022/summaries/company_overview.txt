Visa Inc. is a leading global payments technology company founded in 1958, operating in over 200 countries and territories. It provides secure, reliable, and efficient digital payment solutions for consumers, merchants, financial institutions, and governments. The company’s core business centers on transaction processing through its VisaNet network, offering a wide array of payment products including credit, debit, prepaid, and digital credentials. Visa’s strengths lie in its extensive acceptance footprint, strong brand recognition, and innovative technologies such as contactless payments and tokenization. Its open partnership approach and API capabilities facilitate rapid integration with fintechs and digital platforms. Serving nearly 15,000 financial institutions and millions of merchants worldwide, Visa maintains a dominant market share in electronic payments. The company continues to expand its offerings through strategic acquisitions and technological innovations, positioning itself for growth amid evolving industry trends and increasing digital adoption.
