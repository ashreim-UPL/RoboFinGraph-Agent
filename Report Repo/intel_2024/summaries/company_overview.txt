Intel Corporation, founded in 1968, is a global leader in semiconductor design, manufacturing, and sales, serving diverse markets such as data centers, personal computers, autonomous vehicles, and edge computing. The company is renowned for its advanced process technologies, extensive research and development capabilities, and integrated manufacturing model. Its product portfolio includes high-performance CPUs, GPUs, accelerators, and foundry services, supported by a dominant x86 ecosystem. Intel maintains a strong market position in server and PC segments, competing with industry giants like AMD, NVIDIA, and TSMC. The company is strategically focused on expanding manufacturing capacity through its IDM 2.0 initiative, launching innovative processors, and forming key industry partnerships. With a global presence, Intel continues to capitalize on emerging trends like AI, cloud computing, and 5G, aiming to reinforce its leadership in the evolving semiconductor industry.
