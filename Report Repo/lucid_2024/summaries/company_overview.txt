Lucid Motors, founded in 2007 and based in Newark, California, is a leading electric vehicle manufacturer focused on high-performance, luxury EVs. The company designs, engineers, and produces advanced vehicles such as the Lucid Air and the upcoming Lucid Gravity SUV, emphasizing long range, fast charging, and innovative software features. With proprietary powertrain and battery technologies, Lucid offers industry-leading range of up to 512 miles. Its core markets include North America, Europe, and the Middle East, where it is expanding its retail and service network. The company adopts a vertical integration approach, controlling hardware and software development, and sells directly to consumers. Recent initiatives include launching the Lucid Gravity, expanding manufacturing capacity in Arizona and Saudi Arabia, and forming strategic partnerships. Lucid aims to strengthen its presence in the premium EV segment through technological innovation, international growth, and product diversification.
