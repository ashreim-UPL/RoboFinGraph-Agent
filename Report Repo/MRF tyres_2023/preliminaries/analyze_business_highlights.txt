MRF Limited's tire manufacturing business offers a diverse range of products catering to various vehicle segments, demonstrating its strong presence in both domestic and international markets. The company's tire portfolio includes specialized tires for trucks, passenger cars, off-the-road vehicles, and motorsports, supported by comprehensive services such as wheel alignment and tubeless repairs, which enhance customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. Additionally, MRF's paints and coatings division provides a variety of finishes and coatings, expanding its product offerings beyond tires and contributing to its diversified revenue streams.
