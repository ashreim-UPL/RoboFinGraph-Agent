Company overview: MRF Limited, established in 1946, is a leading Indian multinational tire manufacturer with a strong global presence. The company offers a broad range of rubber products, including tires, tubes, flaps, and tread rubber, serving various vehicle segments such as passenger cars, trucks, buses, two-wheelers, off-the-road vehicles, and specialized applications like military and agricultural equipment. Known for its extensive product portfolio, technological expertise, and customer-centric services like wheel alignment and tubeless repairs, MRF has built a reputation for quality and durability. Its popular tire lines, including MRF PERFINZA, MRF MARKUS, and MRF ZAPPER, are recognized worldwide. The company maintains a robust distribution network and strong relationships with OEMs and aftermarket distributors. Focused on innovation and expansion, MRF continues to invest in R&D, eco-friendly solutions, and strategic partnerships to sustain growth and strengthen its market position both domestically and internationally.
