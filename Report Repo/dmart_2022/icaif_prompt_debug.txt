## company_overview
Avenue Supermarts Limited, operating under the DMart brand since 2000, is a prominent player in India’s organized retail sector. The company runs a network of around 284 supermarkets across major states, catering to a broad customer base that includes middle-income families and small businesses. DMart’s stores offer a comprehensive selection of groceries, FMCG products, apparel, kitchenware, and home appliances, all positioned around its core promise of everyday low prices. Its strengths lie in a strong supply chain, deep local market understanding, and a value-driven approach that fosters customer loyalty. DMart has responded to industry trends by expanding its store footprint, growing its e-commerce platform DMart Ready, and investing in sustainability. These strategies, combined with operational efficiencies, position the company for continued growth in India’s evolving retail landscape.
