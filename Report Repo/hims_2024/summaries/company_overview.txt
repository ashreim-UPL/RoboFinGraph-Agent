Hims & Hers Health, Inc., founded in 2017, is a leading telehealth platform focused on providing accessible, personalized health and wellness solutions. It connects consumers with licensed healthcare professionals across various specialties, including sexual health, hair loss, dermatology, mental health, and weight management. The company offers prescription medications, over-the-counter products, cosmetics, and supplements through online channels and retail partnerships. Its strengths include a proprietary technology platform, an extensive provider network, and clinical expertise that ensure high-quality care. With a focus on subscription-based treatments, curated wellness products, and integrated pharmacy services, Hims & Hers delivers seamless, affordable healthcare. Serving primarily the US and UK markets, the company leverages digital convenience and retail presence to enhance customer engagement. Its innovative approach and broad product portfolio position it as a prominent leader in the rapidly growing telehealth industry.
