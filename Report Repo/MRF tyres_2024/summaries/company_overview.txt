MRF Limited, established in 1960, is a prominent multinational tire manufacturer based in India, serving a wide range of segments including passenger vehicles, trucks, buses, two-wheelers, off-the-road equipment, and military applications. The company boasts a diverse product portfolio featuring flagship tires like MRF PERFINZA, MRF MARKUS, and MRF MOGRIP, supported by strong research and development efforts focused on sustainable innovations such as low rolling resistance and eco-friendly materials. MRF maintains a dominant market position in India and exports to Asia, Africa, and Europe, supplying major OEMs and aftermarket customers. Its competitive advantages stem from a reputation for quality, technological leadership, and strategic focus on innovation. The company is actively expanding into emerging markets, developing green products, and investing in digital and manufacturing efficiencies to sustain growth amid industry shifts toward electric vehicles and sustainability.
