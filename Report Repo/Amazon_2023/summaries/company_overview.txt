Amazon.com, Inc. is a global leader in technology and retail, serving millions of consumers, third-party sellers, enterprises, developers, content creators, and advertisers through its online and physical stores. Founded in 1994, Amazon operates across North America, international markets, and through Amazon Web Services (AWS), maintaining dominant positions in e-commerce and cloud computing. The company’s strengths include a vast product selection, customer-focused innovation, and advanced logistics and technology infrastructure, enabling fast delivery and personalized experiences. Amazon’s business lines—ranging from retail and marketplace services to cloud computing and digital content—have shown robust growth, particularly in North America and AWS. The company continues to invest in fulfillment, technology, and strategic acquisitions, while navigating challenges such as global competition, supply chain issues, and economic pressures in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.
